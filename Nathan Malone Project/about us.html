How we started

We originated in Amsterdam, Netherlands in 1949 crafting clothing for local residents and selling at local markets. Our founder is Nathan Malone who started the company who would go to local markets in the outskirts of amterdam and sell items of clothing to local farmers which consisted mainly of jackets and coats for the winter. It was a solo operation up until 6 months later when the employee count went up to 10. This caused the production rate to multiply and profits start coming in. This process was repeated continuously multiplying the employees in the company to make it what it is today
